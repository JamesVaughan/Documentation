# Length

Returns the number of cells in the given Vector or Matrix.

\begin{equation}
A = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 & 2          \\\\
    3 & -4
\end{bmatrix}, 
B = \begin{bmatrix}
    2 & 4          \\\\
    6 & 8
\end{bmatrix}, 
C = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 \\\\
    3
\end{bmatrix}, 
D = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 & 3
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}

### Length(Matrix) = Scalar

\begin{equation}
Length(A) = 4,
Length(B) = 4
\end{equation}

### Length(Vector) = Scalar

\begin{equation}
Length(C) = 2,
Length(D) = 2
\end{equation}

### Length(Scalar) = ERROR

\begin{equation}
Length(0) = ERROR
\end{equation}
