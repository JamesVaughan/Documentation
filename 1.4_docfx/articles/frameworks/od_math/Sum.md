# Sum

Returns the summation of all elements in the structure.

\begin{equation}
A = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 & 2          \\\\
     3 & -4
\end{bmatrix}, \
B = \begin{bmatrix}
    2 & 4          \\\\
    6 & 8
\end{bmatrix}, 
C = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 \\\\
     3
\end{bmatrix}, 
D = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 & 3
\end{bmatrix}
\end{equation}

### Sum(Matrix) = Scalar

\begin{equation}
Sum(A) = 0
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
Sum(B) = 20
\end{equation}

### Sum(Vector) = Scalar

\begin{equation}
Sum(C) = 2
\end{equation}

\begin{equation}
Sum(D) = 2
\end{equation}

### Sum(Scalar) = ERROR

\begin{equation}
Sum(0) = ERROR
\end{equation}
